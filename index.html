VILLA ENLIGHTENED

The "Villa Enlightened " is a tour that highlights the architectural beauty of this city, embellishing it with light.

For more than forty days, the streets form a circuit of light and color, dressed figures and scenes related to Christmas , traditions and identity of the city

TARGET

For the fourth consecutive year the city of Atlixco is filled with light and color through its amazing Christmas show "La Villa Lit 2015 " .


Until next January 6th, 2016 , attendees can enjoy the architectural beauty of the city is highlighted by decorating light circuit , as well as more than 3000 alluding to the holiday season figures.


Enlightened Villa which aims to highlight the culture and tradition of the region during the festivities, also offers a series of cultural and artistic events of international stature, through the participation of more than 30 groups from countries like Spain , Chile, Kenya, Guatemala , Peru , Ireland , Libya, Colombia and Italy.
